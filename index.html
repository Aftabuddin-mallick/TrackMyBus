About TrackMyBus

TrackMyBus is an intelligent transportation solution that provides real-time tracking of buses, alerts for stops, and driver halt notifications. Designed for students and commuters, it ensures safety, punctuality, and convenience through IoT and AI-based monitoring.

Key Features

🎙️ Real-Time Tracking

View live location of buses with precise GPS data for smooth commuting.

⚠️ Driver Halt Alerts

Get notified when buses stop longer than expected at any station.

🔔 Smart Stop Notification

Receive alerts when your stop is approaching or the bus reaches your station.

🚆AI Integration

AI-driven analytics to predict delays, optimize routes, and improve efficiency.
